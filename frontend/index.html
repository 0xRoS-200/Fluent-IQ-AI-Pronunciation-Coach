Privacy & Data Consent

Before using FluentIQ, please review how we handle your data in compliance with India's DPDP Act 2023:

  • Audio is processed entirely on our server — never stored or saved
  • Audio is deleted instantly from memory after analysis
  • Only extracted text (never audio) is sent to AI for feedback
  • No accounts, no tracking, no cookies — fully anonymous
  • You can request data deletion anytime (nothing to delete)
